FAQs

What are the side effects of taking too much Vitamin D?

Taking excessive amounts of Vitamin D can lead to toxicity, causing symptoms such as nausea, weakness, and kidney damage. Always consult a healthcare provider.

Are supplements necessary if I eat a healthy diet?

While a balanced diet provides most nutrients, supplements may be needed to address deficiencies or specific health goals.

How do I know if a supplement is high quality?

Look for third-party testing certifications, check ingredient transparency, and research trusted brands.
