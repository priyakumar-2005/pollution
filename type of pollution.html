Types of Pollution

Explore the different types of pollution and learn about their causes, effects, and ways to reduce them.

Air Pollution

Air pollution occurs due to harmful gases, dust, or smoke in the atmosphere. It can lead to respiratory diseases and environmental damage.

Water Pollution

Water pollution contaminates rivers, lakes, and oceans through chemicals and plastics. It harms aquatic life and reduces water quality.

Learn More

Soil Pollution

Soil pollution is caused by hazardous waste and pesticides, affecting plant growth and agricultural productivity.

Learn More

Noise Pollution

Noise pollution disrupts human and animal life with excessive sound. It causes stress, hearing loss, and behavioral changes in wildlife.

Learn More

Light Pollution

Light pollution is caused by excessive artificial lighting, obscuring the night sky and disrupting ecosystems.
